About Kerala - God's Own Country

Geography

Kerala, located on India's tropical Malabar Coast, has nearly 600km of Arabian Sea shoreline. It's known for its palm-lined beaches, backwaters, mountain ranges, and wildlife sanctuaries.

Climate

Kerala has a wet maritime tropical climate influenced by seasonal heavy rains from the Southwest and Northeast monsoons. The best time to visit is from September to March.

Cultural Highlights

  • Traditional art forms: Kathakali, Mohiniyattam, Theyyam
  • Ayurveda - the ancient healing system
  • Vibrant festivals: Onam, Vishu, Thrissur Pooram
  • Unique cuisine using coconut, rice, and spices

Must-Visit Destinations

Alleppey Backwaters

Network of canals, lagoons and lakes best explored by houseboat

Munnar

Rolling hills of tea plantations with cool climate

Kovalam

Famous beach with lighthouse and ayurvedic resorts

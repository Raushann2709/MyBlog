7-Day Classic Kerala Tour

Perfect for first-time visitors | Best season: September to March

Day 1: Arrival in Kochi

  • Arrive at Cochin International Airport
  • Half-day Fort Kochi tour: Chinese fishing nets, St. Francis Church, Jewish Synagogue
  • Kathakali dance performance in the evening
  • Overnight in Kochi

Day 2: Kochi to Munnar (130km, 4hrs)

  • Morning drive to Munnar through tea plantations
  • Visit Cheeyappara and Valara waterfalls en route
  • Afternoon at leisure or visit Tea Museum
  • Overnight in Munnar

Travel Tips

  • Carry light cotton clothes and rain gear (if traveling in monsoon)
  • Book houseboats in Alleppey in advance during peak season
  • Try local cuisine: appam with stew, karimeen pollichathu, puttu with kadala
